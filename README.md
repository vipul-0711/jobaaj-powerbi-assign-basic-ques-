

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a one-page interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Google Play Store Apps dataset. The objective is to identify the key factors that contribute to an app's success by analyzing user ratings, installs, reviews, and app categories.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor app performance, understand market trends, and make data-driven product and marketing decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

- Analyze app performance on the Google Play Store.
- Identify high-performing app categories.
- Understand user engagement through ratings and reviews.
- Discover factors influencing app popularity.
- Support product and marketing strategy with data-driven insights.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- ⭐ Average Rating
- 📲 Total Installs
- 💬 Total Reviews
- 📱 Total Apps
- 📂 Total Categories
- 📈 Apps Rated 4.0 and Above

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visuals

- Top 10 Categories by Total Installs (Bar Chart)
- Rating vs Installs (Scatter Plot)
- Free vs Paid Apps (Donut Chart)
- Installs by Content Rating (Column Chart)
- Top 10 Apps by Installs (Table)

---

## 🎛 Interactive Filters

- Category
- App Type (Free/Paid)
- Content Rating

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Converted Installs and Reviews to numeric values
- Converted Price to decimal
- Converted Last Updated to Date format
- Standardized text values

---

## 💼 Business Insights

- Categories with the highest installs indicate strong market demand.
- Apps with ratings above 4.0 generally attract more users.
- Free apps dominate the Google Play Store ecosystem.
- High review counts indicate strong user engagement.
- Content rating influences download trends across different audiences.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used


- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Source:** Google Play Store Apps Dataset (Kaggle)

---

## 🚀 Outcome

This dashboard provides an executive-level overview of app performance, enabling product managers, developers, and business teams to quickly identify successful app categories, monitor key metrics, and make informed decisions.
